📘 Quick First Aid Guides

  • CPR: Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 beats per minute).
  • Bleeding: Apply firm, direct pressure with a clean cloth until bleeding stops.
  • Burns: Cool the burn under running water for at least 10 minutes. Do not apply ice.
  • Choking: Perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver) just above the navel.
